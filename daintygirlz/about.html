A personal decoration worn hanging from the ear, an earring is typically suspended by a ring or hook that passes through the lobe's piercing or, more commonly in modern times, by a screwed clip.

Earrings

The desire to embellish or change how the ear looks seems to be practically universal. Earrings are typically worn in pairs, with the two jewels matching in every way. However, solo earrings have been used on occasion.
